AVYAKT 3.0

Avyakt 3.0, the departmental techno-cultural fest, is a highly anticipated event that brings together the brightest minds and creative talents from various departments and disciplines. With each successive iteration, Avyakt has grown in scale and popularity, solidifying its status as a hallmark event within the academic community. This annual extravaganza showcases the perfect blend of technology and culture, providing a platform for students to not only exhibit their technical prowess but also express their artistic inclinations. Avyakt 3.0 is more than just a festival; it's a celebration of innovation, creativity, and collaboration

DEPARTMENT OF CSE

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is a vibrant hub of innovation and technological advancement within our academic institution. With a strong commitment to excellence in both teaching and research, the CSE department plays a pivotal role in shaping the future of technology.Within the CSE department, students are exposed to a comprehensive curriculum that covers a wide array of computer science topics, from programming fundamentals to cutting-edge developments in artificial intelligence and data science. The department is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories and facilities, fostering a hands-on approach to learning and experimentation.

GIET UNIVERSITY

GIET University believes that quality education should be followed by prime placements. Over the decades, the relentless effort of GIET University has ingrained a reputation for consistent quality placements. It has formed enduring & constructive partnerships with the corporate/Industries to provide foot-in- the-door opportunity to all the eligible students, enabling them to start off their career just right after they complete their course curriculum without facing the real-world job struggle. As a result, GIET University has been crowned the “Best University in Eastern India” for Campus placement at the “Asia Education Summit - 2020”.
